    <h1>Huskfolio Project Overview</h1>
    <dl>
        <dt><big><b>Project Name:</b></big></dt>
        <dd>Huskfolio</dd>
        
        <dt><big><b>URL of the Site’s Homepage:</b></big></dt>
        <dd>Huskfolio.art</dd>
        
        <dt><big><b>Summary of the Website:</b></big></dt>
        <dd>
            Huskfolio will be a portfolio website used to showcase my character art, illustrations, and other creative projects.<br>
            The purpose of it will be to build my online presence and establish my brand.<br>
            It will serve as a hub for potential employers, friends, and fans, providing a platform where visitors can easily explore my work and learn more about my creative journey.
        </dd>
        
        <dt><big><b>Site Objectives:</b></big></dt>
        <dd>
            The main goal is to highlight my skills and attract opportunities for collaborations and employment.<br>
            I also want to keep my audience engaged by frequently updating the site with new content.<br>
            This will make it easier for visitors to access my portfolio and contact me.
        </dd>
        
        <dt><big><b>Target Audience:</b></big></dt>
        <dd>
            Potential employers, collaborators, and fans interested in my art.<br>
            This audience was chosen to align with my goals of showcasing my work to those who might offer professional opportunities or simply appreciate my art.
        </dd>
        
        <dt><big><b>Content Overview:</b></big></dt>
        <dd>
            The website will feature a character art portfolio, an "About Me" section, a blog or updates page, and a contact page.<br>
            Each section is chosen to reflect my passion for art and to engage visitors with my work and personal story.<br>
            The content will be curated to offer a comprehensive view of my artistic abilities and achievements.
        </dd>
        
        <dt><big><b>Design Ideas:</b></big></dt>
        <dd>
            For the design, I plan to use a color scheme of green and grey, since green is my favorite color, to give the site a personal feel.<br>
            The overall feel will be playful and silly to reflect my personality while keeping a user-friendly layout.
        </dd>
        
        <dt><big><b>HTML & CSS Use:</b></big></dt>
        <dd>
            Huskfolio will be built with HTML to organize the content on the site.<br>
            CSS will be used to make the site look good by changing things like colors, fonts, and layout.
        </dd>
        
        <dt><big><b>Importance of Semantic Structure:</b></big></dt>
        <dd>
            Building Huskfolio with the right structure and following web standards is important for making sure the site works well for everyone, no matter what device they’re using.
        </dd>
    </dl>
